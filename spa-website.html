Meet Breanne Capece

Welcome to Spa Rejuvenate, where healing begins with passion and dedication. I'm Breanne Capece, owner and operator of this tranquil sanctuary, and I've been drawn to the art of therapeutic touch since I was a young girl.

My fascination with massage therapy began early in life when I witnessed firsthand how the power of healing hands could transform pain into relief, stress into serenity. This early exposure to the profound impact of therapeutic touch ignited a passion that would shape my entire career path. After years of dedicated study and hands-on training, I established Spa Rejuvenate with a simple yet powerful mission: to create a space where every client can experience the transformative benefits of personalized massage therapy. With over a decade of experience, I specialize in treatments that address your unique needs, promoting both physical healing and mental well-being. At Spa Rejuvenate, we believe that true wellness comes from understanding each client's individual journey. Every session is tailored to your specific needs, ensuring you leave feeling renewed, refreshed, and truly rejuvenated.
